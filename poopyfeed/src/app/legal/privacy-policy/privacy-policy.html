Privacy Policy

1. Overview

PoopyFeed is a baby care tracking application designed for parents and caregivers. We collect and use information to provide, maintain, and improve the service, and to support the well-being of the children you track. This Privacy Policy applies to users located in the United States.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use PoopyFeed:

  • Account information – such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Child profiles – such as a child’s name, date of birth, and optional details you choose to provide.
  • Activity data – such as feedings, diaper changes, naps, and related notes you record in the app.
  • Usage information – such as device type, browser type, approximate location based on IP address, and how you interact with the app.
  • Sharing and invite information – such as email addresses of co‑parents or caregivers you invite and how they use the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the PoopyFeed service.
  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your access.
  • To sync data between co‑parents and caregivers you choose to share with.
  • To personalize your experience, such as showing relevant analytics and summaries.
  • To monitor, maintain, and improve the security and performance of the app.
  • To communicate with you about your account, support requests, and service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service providers – with trusted vendors who help us operate the service (for example, hosting, analytics, or email providers) under contractual obligations to protect your information.
  • Co‑parents and caregivers – with people you explicitly invite or grant access to a child’s profile.
  • Legal and safety – when required by law, subpoena, or similar legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of PoopyFeed, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers – in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data through the in‑app account settings or by contacting us using the details below.

6. Children’s Privacy

PoopyFeed is intended to be used by adults (such as parents and caregivers) to track information about children. The service is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and children are not permitted to create their own accounts.

If we learn that we have collected personal information directly from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information as required by the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable U.S. laws.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

8. Your Choices

Depending on how you use the service, you may be able to:

  • Access and update your account information in the account settings page.
  • Update or delete child profiles and activity entries you have created.
  • Adjust notification or communication preferences where available.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

9. International Users

PoopyFeed is currently designed and operated for users in the United States. If you access the service from outside the U.S., you understand that your information may be processed and stored in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those of your country.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and may provide additional notice as appropriate. Your continued use of PoopyFeed after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
